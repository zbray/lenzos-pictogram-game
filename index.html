Lenzo's Pictogram Game

Welcome! Ganon's minions have wrecked havoc in this region and the townsfolk have been trying to fend them off for months! To do my part I'm offering my favorite Pictograph to a hero with a sharp mind and able strength. If you can win my game, the Pictograph is yours!

Each rupee is worth a different amount of points, chosen at random.

Each time you click on a rupee that rupee's point value will be added to your total.

If you can reach the exact number I choose with the right combination of rupees, you win and the Pictograph is yours!
